tActIc-toe

tActIc-toe lets you create your very own MENACE! You can train your mini-ai by playing against them or making use of the handy Fast Play option. Watch them improve and help them when they plateau by modifying their incentives, rewarding or punishing them more as they gain experience. Finally, you can pit your MENACE against your friends creations in BATTLE!

About M.E.N.A.C.E

Matchbox Educable Noughts And Crosses Engine

In 1961, AI researcher Donald Michie designed MENACE, a mechanical computer made from 304 matchboxes. It was able to play noughts and crosses and refine its strategy through reinforcement learning. After every match, MENACE would review the moves played and every corresponding matchbox would be updated by predefined incentives. In this way, MENACE eliminated losing strategies until it was able to consistently draw and sometimes beat (inexperienced) players. Great resources to learn more about MENACE are:
